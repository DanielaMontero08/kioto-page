Things you should know about kioto...

It is one of the oldest Shinto shrines in Japan, built more than 100 years before Kyoto itself, which it protects from evil influences. One of the Buddhist temples in Kyoto that is still preserved today, its construction took more than 30 years. Kyoto is the perfect city for those who like a slower pace of life. Some of the most important industries in Kyoto are handicrafts and traditional Japanese customs such as kimono production and geisha districts. The city is also famous for its festivals with more than a thousand years of tradition such as the Gion Matsuri which is still celebrated today.
